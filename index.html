Welcome to Bear Creek Projects

‘Bear Creek Projects’ is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming lives through education, mentorship, and community service. Our mission is to empower individuals, especially children and youth, to reach their full potential by providing access to quality educational support and fostering a nurturing and inclusive community. Bear Creek Tutoring Program is a volunteer-driven, member-led initiative and partnership with local Organizations.

An Educational Enrichment initiative (Free Tutoring Service) designed to empower students through academic support, mentorship, and personal growth. Aim to support local students facing academic challenges and align with School District Curriculum...

Bear Creek Tutoring Program

Our flagship program, Bear Creek Tutoring, provides free, personalized tutoring services to students who are in need. Within a two-mile radius of Bear Creek Tutoring Center, there are approximately 1,500 families, each with at least two children. We focus on core subjects such as math, reading, and science, helping students build strong foundational skills.
