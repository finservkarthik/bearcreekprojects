Empower Education, Transform Lives: Join Us for the Bear Creek Tutoring Fundraising Banquet At Bear Creek Projects, we believe in the power of education to change lives. Our Bear Creek Tutoring Program provides free tutoring services to students in need, offering the tools and resources they need to succeed academically and personally.

By joining us for our Inaugural Fundraising Banquet, you’ll be helping us continue this vital work and expand our reach to even more students.

Event Details

Date: Saturday, November 2nd, 2024
Time: 6 PM
Location: Metro Church, 13930 Distribution Way, Farmers Branch, TX 75234

Join us for an evening of engaging activities, inspiring stories, and the opportunity to support our mission. Your presence at this event will help us make a lasting difference in the lives of students and the communities we serve.
